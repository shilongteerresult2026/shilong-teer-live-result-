📜 Shillong Teer History, Tradition & Culture

  • 🏹 Origin & Cultural Significance: Shillong Teer is a living cultural heritage of the Khasi community in Meghalaya. Originating during the British colonial era (1830s-1940s), archery was traditionally a means of self-defense and a royal sport.
  • 🎯 Traditional Rituals: Before each match, local elders perform traditional ceremonies to bless the archers. The game is deeply rooted in Khasi culture.
  • 🏛️ Modern Evolution: Over 200 licensed Teer counters operate across Meghalaya, processing millions of rupees in bets daily.
  • 📌 Did You Know? The oldest recorded Teer match dates back to 1906.
