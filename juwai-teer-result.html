📜 Juwai Teer History, Tradition & Culture

  • 🏹 Origin & Cultural Roots

    Juwai Teer is another regional variant of the traditional archery-based game, played in the Juwai area of Meghalaya. It has its own distinct identity, with local communities celebrating the game as a symbol of unity and cultural pride.

  • 🎯 Traditional Practices

    The Juwai Teer matches are known for their festive atmosphere, with traditional music and dance performances accompanying the competitions. The game has become a social gathering point for families and friends.

  • 🏛️ Current Status

    Juwai Teer operates with the same legal framework as other Teer games in Meghalaya. The timings are FR at 2:30 PM and SR at 3:15 PM, making it one of the afternoon matches.

  • 📌 Did You Know?

    The Juwai Teer grounds are known for their picturesque location, surrounded by lush green hills and traditional Khasi villages.
